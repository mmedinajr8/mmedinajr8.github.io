Biography

My passion in life is road design. At the age of 10 I suudenly became interested in the geography of the world. The way the roads and highways were layed out across entire continents fascinated me. Seeing how I could travel from Miami, Florida all the way to Anchorage, Alaska took my breath away. And with these digital design programs my interest has grown even deeper. Desgning the roads of America would be a dream of mine to accomplish in the future. Civil engineering would be the major I would enter.
